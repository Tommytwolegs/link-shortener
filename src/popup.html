Long URLs on supported sites are rewritten in place. Right-click any link or page and choose "Copy clean URL" for a one-off cleanup, or use "Copy link address" on Amazon to get the clean form directly. The "Universal tracking strip" toggle (off by default) strips common trackers like utm_* from URLs on every site you visit.

Tip: Ctrl+Shift+L (⌘⇧L on Mac) copies a clean URL of the current page. Rebind it in your browser's extension shortcut settings.

Selected a URL as text? Right-click it for "Copy clean URL from selection". Or in the address bar, type clean, press Tab, and paste a URL to jump to its clean form.
