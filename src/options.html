Universal tracking strip

These settings only matter when the Universal tracking strip toggle is on. Per-site cleanup (Amazon, eBay, etc.) is unaffected.

Skip these domains

Hostnames you list here are ignored by the Universal tracking strip — no params will be removed on these sites. Per-site cleanup still runs. Enter one per line, no protocol or path. Wildcard subdomains are supported via a leading dot: .example.com matches example.com and any subdomain. Bare example.com matches only the exact host.

Always keep these parameters

Names listed here are never stripped, even when the Universal tracking strip would otherwise remove them. Matching is case-insensitive. Enter one parameter name per line, no = sign or value.

About

Jimothy's Link Shortener stores your toggle preferences in chrome.storage.sync so they follow you across Chrome installs signed in to the same Google account. Nothing is sent over the network — the extension makes zero remote calls.
